of electromagnetic waveforms across multiple frequency bands. It uses adaptive filtering to
isolate signal components and extract meaningful patterns from ambient noise.

The parallel waveform synthesizer complements the detector by generating calibrated reference
signals used during sensor calibration. Together, these systems form the foundation of the
LocalLoom spectral analysis module.
